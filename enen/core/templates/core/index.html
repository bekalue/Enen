    Inspiration for creating the Enen web application came from the developer's passion to improve the lives of Ethiopian people through technology. He wanted to make a positive impact on the healthcare system in Ethiopia by providing easy access to medical assistance and health information. His goal is to change and improve the healthcare system in Ethiopia, making it more accessible and convenient for everyone.
